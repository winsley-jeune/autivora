## 2. Where & How to Find Prospects (free methods)

- **Google Maps lists** — Search "boutique hotel / day spa / pilates studio + [city]." Build a sheet of name, address, website, and the owner/manager if listed. Sort by photos that show a polished interior (they invest in experience = they'll invest in scent).
- **Instagram local business** — Search local hashtags (#[city]spa, #[city]boutique) and the "Places" tab. DM businesses with strong aesthetic feeds; they already care about brand experience.
- **LinkedIn** — Filter by title (Owner, GM, Spa Director, Studio Manager) + geography. Best for hotels, clinics, co-working, and multi-location groups.
- **Walk-ins** — The highest-conversion channel for a premium product. Visit 5-10 businesses in one district; you literally let them smell the difference. Bring a sell sheet.
- **Local Facebook & Nextdoor groups** — "[City] Small Business Owners," BNI/networking groups. Offer value first; don't spam-post.
- **Chambers of commerce** — Member directories are free prospect lists, pre-sorted by location and category. Attend one mixer a month.
- **Cross-referrals** — Interior designers, commercial cleaners, and salon-supply reps already sell to your ICPs; one relationship = a warm pipeline.

**Tip:** Keep one simple spreadsheet — Business, Segment, Contact, Channel, Last touch, Status. That sheet *is* your sales engine.

## 9. Weekly Cadence & KPIs

**Weekly playbook (realistic for a solo/bootstrapped founder):**

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| **Mon** | Build/refresh list — add 25 new prospects to the sheet |
| **Tue** | Send 15 Email 1s + 5 Instagram/LinkedIn DMs |
| **Wed** | **Walk-ins** — visit 8–10 businesses in one district, sell sheet in hand |
| **Thu** | Send Email 2 follow-ups (to last week's non-responders) |
| **Fri** | Send Email 3 breakups; log replies; book any demos/calls |

**Follow-up timing:** Email 1 → +3 days Email 2 → +5 days Email 3. Then move "later" replies to a quarterly check-back list. Never more than 3 emails per prospect.

**Weekly volume target:** ~75 fresh touches (50 email/DM + ~10 walk-in + follow-ups).

**3 KPIs to track:**
1. **Reply rate** — replies ÷ first-touches sent (target ~10%+; <5% means fix the subject line or list quality).
2. **Demos/trials booked** — units placed on 30-day try (the real leading indicator of revenue).
3. **Trial → paid conversion** — trials that keep the unit and start a month-to-month oil plan (the number that compounds).

**Rule of thumb:** Walk-ins convert highest because they let the prospect *smell* the product — weight your time there once email fills the top of the funnel.
