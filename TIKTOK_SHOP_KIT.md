# Autivara TikTok Shop Kit

**The thesis:** TikTok Shop is the single best-fit channel for Autivara, and we're early enough that it's still rewarding. Three reasons:

1. **Product fit is perfect.** Flame, mist, disco-light, jellyfish — these are *made* for satisfying short-form video, and sub-$50 price points are exactly the impulse-buy zone where TikTok Shop converts.
2. **It merges content + commerce.** The Reels we're already shooting (see `SOCIAL_PLAYBOOK.md`) become directly shoppable — no "link in bio" friction. View → tap → buy, in-app.
3. **Early-adopter leverage.** The creator-affiliate marketplace still has runway, new-seller fee incentives exist, and the algorithm pushes Shop-tagged content. Getting reviews + GMV now compounds.

> **How this changes the plan:** TikTok Shop becomes our **primary marketplace** (replacing Amazon as the priority — Amazon stays as documented in `AMAZON_LISTINGS.md` for *phase 2*, once we have trademark/Brand Registry/GTINs). Social and "marketplace" are now **one motion**, not two.

---

## 1. The big unlock: the Creator Affiliate program

This is why TikTok Shop fits a bootstrapped brand better than Amazon PPC: **you don't pay upfront for traffic.** You list products in the affiliate marketplace, set a commission, and creators make videos selling your product — you only pay commission *on sales*. You also get a flood of UGC for free.

- **Open Plan** — any creator can promote at a commission % you set (start ~15–20%).
- **Targeted Plan** — invite specific creators with a higher rate.
- **Free sample program** — approve sample requests; a creator gets a unit, posts, and their audience buys. One viral video can do more than weeks of your own posting.

**For Autivara specifically:** seed the visually-loud SKUs (Ember flame, Nova disco, Nimbus jellyfish) to home/aesthetic/car micro-creators. The product *demos itself* — that's the whole advantage.

## 2. Setup checklist

| Step | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Seller eligibility** | TikTok Shop US generally requires a **registered US business + EIN** and a US bank account. *Confirm current individual-seller eligibility* — it changes. (You already sell via Shopify, so most of this likely exists.) |
| **TikTok Shop Seller Center** | Register at **seller-us.tiktok.com**. Submit business docs + tax info. Link your `@autivarashop` account. |
| **Connect Shopify** | Install the **TikTok Shop** app in Shopify → syncs your catalog and **auto-imports orders for fulfillment** (critical: keeps one inventory + fulfillment source, since the storefront is already Shopify). |
| **Product catalog** | Sync the hero SKUs first (don't dump all 19). Each needs: clean white-background main image, TikTok-style title, price, stock. Use the `AMAZON_LISTINGS.md` titles/bullets as a starting point — but rewrite shorter/punchier for TikTok (see §4). |
| **Fulfillment** | Seller-fulfilled to start (you ship). Evaluate **Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT)** later if volume justifies it. |
| **Compliance** | Product images on white, accurate descriptions, no fabricated claims (same rules as Amazon). Diffuser category is allowed; avoid medical/therapeutic claims about the oils. |
| **Reviews** | Reviews carry over per product and drive conversion — prioritize getting the first few via honest buyers + affiliate sales. |

## 4. Content → commerce playbook

Same shooting discipline as the social playbook, but every video now **tags the product** so the "yellow basket" shows.

**Video formula that sells on TikTok Shop:**
1. **Hook in 1s** — the effect, full-frame (flame ignites / disco light fills a dark room).
2. **Satisfying middle** — 5–8s of the visual, trending audio.
3. **Soft reason-to-buy** — on-screen text: "refillable • no cartridges • $44."
4. **Tag the product** — pin it to the video; add a CTA sticker.

**Formats to run:**
- **Shoppable videos** — 3–4/week, repurposed from the social calendar, product tagged.
- **Product Showcase** — keep all hero SKUs on your profile's Shop tab.
- **LIVE shopping** — once you have a few products + reviews, run a weekly LIVE: demo each diffuser, drop limited-time LIVE-only offers. LIVE is where early adopters win big — most small brands aren't doing it yet.

## 5. Affiliate program setup (the engine)

| Lever | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **Open Plan commission** | Start **15–20%**. High enough that creators pick you over a stingier brand; your premium margin absorbs it (and there's no upfront ad cost). |
| **Targeted Plan** | Hand-pick 10–20 home-decor / car / aesthetic micro-creators (5k–50k followers convert best). Offer **20–25%** + a free sample. |
| **Sample seeding** | Approve sample requests for the hero SKUs. Budget ~10–20 units/month as your "ad spend" — except it produces owned UGC + sales, not just impressions. |
| **Briefs** | Give creators a one-line brief + the hooks from the social playbook, but let them riff — native beats scripted on TikTok. |

**Target creator niches:** #roomtransformation, #aesthetic, #cartok / #carmusthaves, #amazonfinds-style "cool gadgets," #momsoftiktok (jellyfish/nightlight), #dormtok (disco/jellyfish).

## 6. Pricing & fees (protect the premium)

- **TikTok Shop commission/referral fee** is low relative to Amazon (historically mid-single-digit %, with **reduced new-seller promo rates** — *confirm current rate in Seller Center*). Plus your **affiliate commission** (15–25%) only on affiliate-driven sales.
- **Your sub-$50 SKUs work here** *because* TikTok is impulse-driven — but hold the line on value-based pricing. Don't discount to the floor; use **LIVE-only / limited-time** offers and **bundles** (diffuser + oil) to create urgency without permanently cutting price.
- Keep **price parity** with the DTC site; reserve deepest value for DTC bundles/subscription.

## 7. 30-day launch sequence

| Days | Focus |
|---|---|
| **1–7** | Register Seller Center; connect Shopify app; sync the 5 hero SKUs with clean images + titles; set up Product Showcase on the profile. |
| **8–14** | Launch the **Open Plan affiliate** (15–20%); post 3–4 shoppable videos (repurpose social Week-1 clips with product tagged); approve first sample requests. |
| **15–21** | Hand-pick + invite 10–20 micro-creators to a **Targeted Plan** with samples; keep posting; reply to every comment. |
| **22–30** | Run your **first LIVE** (demo all hero SKUs + a LIVE-only bundle); double down on whichever SKU/creator is converting; collect reviews. |

**KPIs:** affiliate sign-ups, GMV, video → product CTR, and creator-content volume. Watch which SKU + which creator combo converts — then pour samples there.
