# TikTok Shop — Required Product Attributes

TikTok Shop rejects products until category-required attributes are filled (these don't sync from Shopify). Fill them in **Seller Center → the product → Additional product information**. Use the **category template** ("Use category templates… Create") to fill once and apply across products.

> **Two fields only YOU can supply — never guess:**
> - **Shipping weight + dimensions** — measure the actual boxed product. Wrong values = overcharged shipping or rejected orders.
> - **CA Prop 65 compliance** — a legal attestation (see §4). Your call, ideally with supplier documentation.

---

## 1. Universal values (same for every Autivara product)

| Field | Value |

| **Recommended Age** | Adults *(or "16 years and up")* — these are not children's toys. |

## 4. Compliance (REQUIRED — your legal attestation)

**CA Prop 65 (Reproductive Chemicals + Carcinogens):** California requires a warning if a product can expose users to listed chemicals (common in imported electronics, PVC cords, plastics).
- If you have **supplier documentation/testing** showing the product is free of listed substances → you may answer **No**.
- If you **don't have that proof** → the conservative, common practice is to answer **Yes** and apply the standard Prop 65 warning. It's cheap insurance against California penalties; the trade-off is a small warning label on the listing.
- **This is a legal decision — make it deliberately, ideally after asking your supplier for a Prop 65 / compliance statement.** Don't reflexively click "No."

**Dangerous Goods / Hazmat:** No for non-battery units; **Yes** for built-in-lithium units (Cabin, Pulse). See §2.

**Contains Batteries or Cells:** None for USB/plug units; **Cells** for rechargeable units; **Batteries** if a remote with a replaceable cell is included.

## 5. Category & title tips

- **Re-categorize Nova (and the other diffusers) to an aroma-diffuser / humidifier category** rather than "Lighting" — it's more accurate, improves discovery, and asks for more relevant attributes. (TikTok auto-picked Lighting because of the disco-ball; override it.)
- **Title:** TikTok favors plain, keyword-front-loaded titles over the brand-em-dash style. For Nova, consider:
  > *Autivara Disco Ball Diffuser – Cool Mist Aromatherapy with Rotating Party Light, Refillable Essential Oil Humidifier for Bedroom & Game Room*
  (Drop the "—" em dash; lead with what people search.) The `AMAZON_LISTINGS.md` titles/bullets are a good source — they're written keyword-first.
